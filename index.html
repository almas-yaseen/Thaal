    Moroccan Masala Shawai

    A light Moroccan Masala with a kick of spice.

    Flavorful and Irresistible! Introducing our Moroccan Masala Shawai, an exclusive at Thaal Kitchen! This chef's special features tender, grilled chicken (Shawai) infused with our signature Moroccan spices. The dish is elevated with a hint of buttery richness and tossed with vibrant chilly flakes for a spicy kick. Juicy tomatoes bring a fresh tang, while roasted garlic adds a deep, aromatic warmth. Each bite offers a perfect balance of smoky, spicy, and savory flavors, capturing the essence of Moroccan cuisine in a truly unforgettable way.


    Dragon Alfaham

    a bold and fiery twist on traditional alfaham.

    Infused with smoky, char-grilled flavors! Presenting the Dragon Alfaham, a bold and fiery twist on traditional alfaham that’s sure to awaken your taste buds! This Thaal Kitchen special showcases tender chicken, marinated to perfection in a unique blend of spices and fiery dragon sauce. Infused with smoky, char-grilled flavors and a touch of heat, the chicken is slow-grilled to achieve a perfect balance of tenderness and spice. Each bite brings a wave of flavors, with a hint of sweetness and a spicy kick, making this dish an adventurous delight for those who crave bold, unforgettable flavors!


    Mixed Chattichoru

    This indulgent Kerala-style meal

    Introducing the Mixed Chattichoru, Thaal Kitchen’s ultimate one-pot feast! This indulgent Kerala-style meal combines fragrant rice with an array of flavors and textures, including succulent seafood, tender chicken, and a variety of Kerala’s finest curries. Nestled in a traditional clay pot (chatti), this hearty meal features juicy prawns, crispy fried fish, and spiced chicken, layered with stir-fried vegetables, tangy pickles, creamy yogurt, and crunchy papadam. Each bite is a taste journey through Kerala, enriched with a dash of ghee and aromatic spices that make this dish an unforgettable treat for seafood and meat lovers alike!
